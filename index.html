Urgent Flood Relief in Sylhet Bangladesh

Prices of all food items necessary for immediate relief assistance in flood-affected areas in Sylhet have seen a sudden spike. Prices of puffed rice, flattened rice, and molasses have increased the most among dry foods. Additionally, prices of accessories, such as candles, have increased too, reports our Sylhet correspondent.

Medicine for flooded area

After the floods, it is important to look at replacing medicines that have been in contact with flood water or other contaminated water. This includes pills, liquids, injections, inhalers or creams. Contact your doctor or pharmacist as soon as possible and organise replacement medications.
